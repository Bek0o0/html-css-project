About us

The IT Career Manchester is a charity, it has been made to guide the refugee and asylum seekers to start your career in IT, this charity is for asylum seekers and refugee who are looking for help to get an IT job in UK, we will work to help you with your plan to do that and connect you with the organisations, which are help you to build your career in IT for free and they also provide you advises and help you with your life in UK as a refugee of asylum seeker. We will help you to decide which track and why. How to apply to universities, which one and why. How to apply for job and will give you advises to your job interview.
